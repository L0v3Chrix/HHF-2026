## Client Overview

Heart Forward Foundation is a nonprofit providing:
- Recovery living scholarships for people in early recovery
- Harm reduction resources and education
- Community events and workshops

---

## Project Goals

### Primary Objectives
1. **Support Journey:** Enable visitors to check eligibility and apply for scholarships
2. **Get Involved Journey:** Enable donations, volunteering, and partnerships
3. **Safety Routing:** Connect visitors to Help Now ATX for out-of-scope needs

### Success Metrics
- Scholarship applications submitted
- Donation completions
- Volunteer/partner inquiries
- Resource page engagement

---

## Technical Requirements

### Must Have
- [x] Mobile-first responsive design
- [x] Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- [x] Fast performance (Lighthouse 90+)
- [ ] Form submission handling
- [ ] Stripe donation integration (placeholder)
- [ ] Analytics tracking (GA4)

### Nice to Have
- [ ] Partner landing page templates
- [ ] Share panels on thank-you pages
- [ ] Event RSVP functionality

---

## Design Direction

### Visual Style
- Dark, elegant aesthetic with warm accents
- Glass morphism / soft cards
- Large serif headlines (Playfair Display)
- Clean sans body text (Inter)
- Deep red/maroon brand color

### Key Components
- Two-door flip cards (homepage)
- Multi-step forms with stepper
- Trust indicators and badges
- Partner lockup variations
